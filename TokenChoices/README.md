# TokenChoices Implementation Instructions

The TokenChoices tool enables consumers to exercise transparency and control over the use of tokenized identifiers in interest-based advertising (IBA). These identifiers may include hashed email addresses or hashed phone numbers used by participating companies within digital advertising environments.

TokenChoices provides mechanisms for consumers to opt out of interest-based advertising associated with these identifiers or to express preferences related to advertising interest categories.

The [TokenChoices Implementation document](TokenChoices%20Implementation.md) provides instructions for how participating companies can integrate with the TokenChoices tool.
 
## Key Concepts

TokenChoices supports two consumer choice mechanisms:

- **Token Opt-Out / Consent Revocation**: Allows a consumer to request that participating companies stop collecting, using, or transferring data associated with a submitted token identifier for interest-based advertising.

- **Category Preferences**: Allows a consumer to signal preferences related to categories of advertising interests associated with a token identifier.

These mechanisms operate using tokenized identifiers such as hashed email addresses or hashed phone numbers.

TokenChoices complements the Digital Advertising Alliance’s broader consumer choice framework, which includes WebChoices (for browser cookies) and AppChoices (for mobile advertising identifiers).

### Purpose
Provides consumers with a destination to:

- Identify participating companies that have committed to responsible advertising practices under the DAA Self-Regulatory Principles.
- Submit tokenized identifiers (e.g., hashed email addresses or hashed phone numbers) associated with digital advertising activity.
- Opt out of the collection and use of those identifiers for interest-based advertising from one, several, or all listed companies.
- Express category-level advertising preferences associated with those identifiers.

### Tool Access
TokenChoices is accessible through the Digital Advertising Alliance's [control page](https://youradchoices.com/control).
